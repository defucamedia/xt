CHRISTOPHER XIAO

Vancouver, BC

Music Composer.

At only 21 years of age, Christopher is the youngest composer ever to have original music entered into shortlists at Berlin, Cannes, Toronto and Venice International Film Festivals, among many others.

He specializes in composing for projects that require a big-budget, Hollywood, and Academy-Award winning sound. He composes music for the symphonic orchestra, as well as an assortment of eletronic and unconventional instruments. He is equipped with a state-of-the-art studio and has access to a full ensemble of musicians.

He holds degrees in History and Economics, speaks German, French, English, Spanish, Mandarin and Cantonese. He voraciously aspires to win an Oscar before the age of 25.
